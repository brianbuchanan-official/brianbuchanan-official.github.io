StreamVerse ‚Äî SaaS Churn & Revenue Analytics

End-to-end subscription analytics case study using a simulated Excel dataset and a Power BI dashboard. KPIs include churn rate, MRR, ARPU, and LTV with segmentation by region, plan type, and family status.

Business Problem

StreamVerse operates in a competitive streaming market where acquisition costs are high. Retention is critical, but monthly churn was eroding MRR and long-term customer value. The objective was to identify churn patterns, quantify revenue risk, and surface high-risk segments.

KPIs

  • Active Customers ‚Äî Paying subscribers each month.
  • Cancels ‚Äî Subscriptions lost per month.
  • Churn Rate (%) ‚Äî Cancels √∑ Active Customers.
  • MRR ‚Äî Monthly subscription revenue.
  • ARPU ‚Äî MRR √∑ Active Customers.
  • LTV ‚Äî ARPU √∑ Churn Rate.

Data & Method

A 12-month synthetic dataset was built in Excel to mimic real SaaS behavior: customers were assigned plan types, regions, add-ons, and churn probabilities that decay over time. The dataset was imported into Power BI, modeled in a star schema, and analyzed using DAX measures.

Core DAX Measures

Active_Customers = SUM(Fact_Monthly[Is_Active])

Cancels = SUM(Fact_Monthly[Churn_Flag])

Churn_Pct = DIVIDE([Cancels], [Active_Customers])

MRR_Monthly = SUM(Fact_Monthly[MRR])

ARPU = DIVIDE([MRR], [Active_Customers])

LTV = DIVIDE([ARPU], [Churn_Pct])

Findings

  • Churn is highest in Month 1 and declines sharply by Month 3.
  • MRR trends upward month-over-month as retention stabilizes.
  • Premium plans retain better and drive higher ARPU and LTV.
  • Segments by Region and Has Kids show meaningful churn differences.

Recommendations

  • Offer annual-plan incentives to reduce month-to-month churn.
  • Improve early-life support and onboarding to reduce Month-1 churn spike.
  • Upsell engaged Basic users into Premium features to increase LTV.
  • Monitor high-risk regions with targeted retention campaigns.

Next Steps

  • Build a Python churn-prediction model to flag high-risk users early.
  • A/B test retention offers to find the most cost-effective approach.
  • Add cohort curves and survival analysis for deeper retention modeling.
